about

Who is the Adaptive Sampling Group (ASG)?
We are master forgers - VFX engineers and computational neuroscientists - leveraging classical optics, computer graphics and digital forensics, in concert with latest forgery and manipulation techniques, to address the rise of doctored content.
How and what does ASG investigate?
With an unbiased view of the content involved, our only interest is the signal information encoded in the image. We are currently focused on academia, journalism and legal applications, but open to discussion with all industries which require expertise.
What services are offered?

Is WITNESS ready for deployment?
Yes - data transport, metadata extraction, pixel statistics, error level analysis, FFT, PCA projection, and more are all in production use. We release iterative builds to maintain a constant development cycle, adhering to the evolving nature of adversarial methodologies.
